Located on the coast of the Adriatic Sea, between the cities of Silvi Marina and Pineto, the tower of Cerrano is an ancient Costal Tower once guarding the city of Pineto, a 20th-century seaside resort on the Adriatic coast, with sandy beaches shaded by pine trees that give their name to the town.
